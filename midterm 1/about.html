About

All Point Financial, Inc. was founded to provide solutions and efficiencies to businesses. Market opportunities appear quickly and require detailed analysis to determine their viability. Businesses must allocate time and resources to such projects without knowing the viability of the opportunity upfront. All Point is there to make this process more efficient by being the resource for analyzing new opportunities and presenting a coherent package to senior management for evaluation. In this manner senior management and other company resources are able to focus on the key activities of the business rather than the first stages of opportunities that may or may not come to fruition. The critical question is always what is the next step. All Point can provide services to help roll-out new programs and execute new opportunities.
