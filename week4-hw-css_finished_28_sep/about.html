Honda S2000 History

About

The incredible S2000 was made by Honda, from 1999 to 2009. The car was launched on April 15, 1999 for Honda's 50th anniversary. The S2000 is named for its engine displacement of two liters. Officially two generations exist: the AP1 and the AP2. Production of the S2000 ended on August 19th 2009. The Honda S2000 was legendary for it's handling and 9k redline.
